Combat Summary

About

The combat summary will pop up after each skirmish you have with another person or team. You will be shown a detailed account of what happened which includes how much damage you inflicted, how many headshots were landed and how many kills were made! The summary will appear approximately 10 seconds after each attacking or killing an enemy.

Damage Inflicted

Damage inflicted will be displayed in a blue value.

Headshot

Headshots landed will be displayed in an orange value.

Kill

Targets killed will be displayed in a red value.
